Fantasy Football Forecast

Patrick Mahomes is someone you want to stay away from. You always talk about value picks, yet Mahomes is the number one projected QB and is sure to regress from last year. Not only do the Chiefs have to play one of the toughest schedules in this league in 2019, but Mahomes will have less talented weapons at his disposal. Tyreek Hill is sure to miss many games and Damien Williams/Carlos Hyde won't be able to make up for Kareem Hunt's unique talent he displayed before being released from the team last year.

Which brings me to Mecole Hardman. Although Hardman isn't even close to the player Tyreek Hill is (or rather was), there will be plenty of opportunity for him to to soak up a good chunk of Hill's targets, being that he has the same playing style and that Mahomes will continue to spread the ball around in Andy Reid's impressive aerial attack.
